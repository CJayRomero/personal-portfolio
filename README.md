# 🌐 Personal Portfolio Website

Hey there! 👋  
Welcome to my **personal portfolio website**, a space where I showcase who I am, what I do, and the creative projects I’ve worked on.  
Built with **React + Vite**, this site serves as my digital hub — combining creativity, design, and technology into one smooth experience.

---

## 🎯 Why I Built This
I wanted a place where I could organize my **skills, works, and achievements** in a single interactive platform.  
It’s like a **digital resume**, but more dynamic — allowing people to know me not just through words, but through design and interactivity.

---

## 🧭 What’s Inside

### 🧍 About Me  
A short introduction about who I am, my passions, and my creative background.

### 🎨 Projects  
A gallery of my works — each project includes visuals, descriptions, and links to live demos or GitHub repos.

### 🛠 Skills  
A snapshot of the tools, programming languages, and creative software I use.

### 📄 Resume  
A downloadable PDF of my resume, summarizing my experience and technical expertise.

### 📬 Contact  
A simple way to reach out — includes my email and links to my professional profiles like LinkedIn and GitHub.

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies Used

- **React** — for building reusable, interactive UI components  
- **Vite** — for fast local development and optimized builds  
- **CSS** — for smooth animations and a clean, responsive design  
- **GitHub Pages** — for easy hosting and deployment  

---

## ✨ Cool Features

- 🖥 **Fully Responsive** — looks great on any device  
- 🎡 **Smooth Transitions** — with Framer Motion animations  
- 🌗 **Dark & Light Mode** — customizable theme toggle  
- 🔗 **Easy Navigation** — simple, sticky header with active page highlights  
- 🎨 **Animated Gradient Background** — adds visual depth and personality
